Accepting that we cannot see everything

-- The Rokeby Venus

“She knew that she was beautiful, that people were looking at her, all of that, it was self-evident. The presence of the visitors standing before her did not disturb her. There was no sign that she was anxious. She reclined calmly, making no gesture. She could have been herself comfortable in other ways, without bearing more, calculating marks, but, in any case, the result was equally perfect ......”
